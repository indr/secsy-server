Your secure and private address book with optional contact information sharing

addressbook lets you anonymously manage your contacts with respect to your privacy. All your information is protected and always sent encrypted over the wire and stored on the server. We've got what you need!

addressbook has everything you need to keep in touch with your friends, family and business peers.

Manage your contacts, add personal notes, labels and location information to see on a map where your contacts are currently located. Opt-in to secure contact information sharing, send and receive updates for your address book entry.
Everything secured and under your control!

addressbook is different. Unlike all the other social networks like Facebook, Google+ or LinkedIn, we respect your privacy. In fact, we're not even able to spy on your data thanks to client-side encryption. Unless you opt-in for secure contact information sharing, we are not even able to find out with whom you are connected!
